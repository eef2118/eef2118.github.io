About

Hosted this year at NYC’s Tribeca 360, The LAVCA Venture Investors Annual Meeting is a one-day event designed to connect the growing community of venture investors in Latin America with international VCs, strategics, and tech companies, for a day of inspiring keynotes and practical discussions.

This is a dedicated VC and early stage investing program that offers a unique environment for global venture capital firms now backing startups in Latin America, along with leading Latin American investors, angels, and accelerators.

Sessions

LAVCA Ventures Investors Annual Summit combines a conversational format and experience-based discussions for an interactive curriculum from local and international markets.
