Latin American PE Deal Book & ESG Cases Third Edition

LAVCA and EY published the third edition of The Latin American Private Equity Deal Book & ESG Cases, highlighting 11 investments ranging across stages, countries, and sectors. Deals include consumer/retail, agribusiness, services, education, oil & gas, and more. Access the latest deal book, as well as past editions, totaling over 35 deals here. Access 2009-2015 Historical Data Excel

2017 LAVCA Industry Data Survey

LAVCA is in the final stretch of collecting data for the annual survey on PE/VC activity in Latin America. Aggregate findings will be featured in 2017 LAVCA Industry Data and Analysis and stats are regularly cited in global and Latin American media (The Wall Street Journal, Financial Times, New York Times, etc.) 200 fund managers participate in LAVCA data collection, which is a key element to promoting capital flows into the region and helps to educate investors about the private equity and venture capital industry in Latin America. If you are a fund manager active in the region and would like to participate in the survey, please contact Natalia Valencia or Eduardo Roman.
